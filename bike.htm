About Bike Co-Op:

During the academic year, the Bike Co-Op on the Downtown Phoenix, Polytechnic and Tempe campuses provides students with the tools and instruction they need to keep their bikes running smoothly and efficiently. They sell routine maintenance items and have many used spare parts that are available for those who need them. They can also order virtually any part you need for your bike, from good-quality economical parts to high-end, high-performance equipment, at prices lower than you can find at most other bike shops. They will show you how to perform repairs and upgrades on your bike for free, and use of any of the tools is also free.

 

Bike Co-Op Services:

  • Bike Adjustments/Repairs
  • Skateboard Adjustments/Repairs
  • Rentals/Other

Bike Adjustments/Repairs:

- FREE FLAT FIX

  • thorn removal
  • new tube
  • rim strip
  • tube sealant
  • check other tires for thorns / correct pressure

- FREE TUNE-UPS

  • break adjustment
  • cable housing and chain cleaning / lube
  • bike wipe down
  • wheel truing (if needed)
  • shifter/derailleur adjustment
  • safety check all components and bolt torque

- BREAK REPAIR

  • rotor truing
  • cable and housing lube
  • disc brake bleeding
  • brake pad adjustment

- WHEEL/TIRE ADJUSTMENTS

  • true wheels
  • hub adjust / rebuild
  • spoke replacement
  • tire / tube / sealant replacement

- SHIFTERS/DERAILLEUR & DRIVETRAIN TUNING

  • shifter & derailleur lube / adjustment
  • cable & housing lube / adjustment
  • chain / cassette / chain ring cleaning

- Free rental bikes for weekly or semester checkout (See Rentals/Other section below)
